bedshape
========

*bedshape* is a wrapper around Steven Busan's `shapemapper2`_ for transcriptome
or genomic wide pre-aligned reads. bedshape is directly inspired by its
predecessor *quickShape* by Evonne McArthur.

.. _shapemapper2: https://github.com/Weeks-UNC/shapemapper2
